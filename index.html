Cross Government Digital Resilience Community

Welcome

We are a group of technologists working in digital resilience across government, some of us are hands on developers, others working in other technical or management roles.

Join our Mailing list

Our Vision

To provide a collaborative forum for technologists across government to work on improving digital resiliency.

  • To learn and to teach
  • To share ideas
  • To have some fun

Our vision helps us fullfil our Objectives.

How are we doing this

  • Engaging with the community on UK Government Digital Slack - in the #resilience channel
  • Running monthly sessions
  • Looking to run more events and meetups
  • We have a small facilitation group that is doing some of the initial setup

Join us!

  • Join the community by signing up to our mailing list
  • Chat with us in #resilience in cross-gov slack.
